About Atomic Frenzy

Atomic Frenzy LLC was established on November 1, 2020 as an independent, nonprofit research organization. We are headquartered in Rutland, Vermont, and operate out of a dedicated laboratory unit behind a commercial storefront on Shopping Plaza Road.

Our organization was founded to advance the understanding of analytical chemistry and formulation science. We concentrate on the stability, solubility and physicochemical properties of small‑molecule compounds, using modern analytical techniques and methodical experimentation to generate reliable data and insights.

Atomic Frenzy operates as a private research institute. Our nonprofit, research‑driven focus distinguishes us from contract testing laboratories, manufacturers and health care institutions.

Our work is conducted exclusively for internal research purposes and is not intended for diagnostic, therapeutic or clinical use. We operate under the direction of experienced scientists and maintain compliance with all applicable safety and regulatory guidelines.

Use of Research Products

Atomic Frenzy purchases reagents and reference standards for research use only. We fully adhere to Cayman Chemical's policy for research products, which states that unless otherwise indicated, products are not for human or veterinary disease diagnosis or therapeutic drug use【13413615967985†L123-L126】. Such products should be used only by technically qualified individuals or those under their direct supervision, and users should review and understand the relevant Safety Data Sheets (SDS) prior to use【13413615967985†L123-L131】.

Our laboratory work is strictly non‑clinical, and we do not administer any materials to humans or animals. All research is conducted within the scope of our non‑profit research mission. The data generated is used internally and shared with the scientific community only in aggregated or anonymised form, without revealing specific compound identities or proprietary methods.

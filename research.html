Our Research Focus

Atomic Frenzy undertakes carefully designed experiments to examine how small‑molecule compounds behave under different conditions. Our goal is to provide actionable data on the formulation and stability of these compounds without divulging proprietary information or specific formulations. Our current initiatives include:

  • Dissolution profiling – evaluating the dissolution characteristics of compounds in various solvent systems【382446765802573†L57-L61】. Understanding solubility and dissolution rates is essential for predicting how compounds will perform in different environments.
  • Stability assessments – assessing compound stability under varying storage conditions【382446765802573†L60-L63】. We monitor changes in physical and chemical properties over time to determine optimal storage strategies.
  • Solubility and precipitation analysis – characterizing solubility limits, precipitation tendencies and degradation behaviour【382446765802573†L60-L65】. This work helps ensure compounds remain suitable for their intended research applications.

Our research is non‑clinical and non‑diagnostic. We conduct these studies under controlled laboratory conditions with qualified personnel and do not release data that could be used for therapeutic or diagnostic purposes.

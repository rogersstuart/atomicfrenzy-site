Advancing Small‑Molecule Science

Atomic Frenzy LLC is a private research institute focusing on pharmaceutical sciences and analytical chemistry. Our mission is to understand the formulation, stability and properties of small‑molecule compounds and to translate this knowledge into insights for the broader scientific community.

*Atomic Frenzy operates strictly as a research organization. Our work is non‑clinical, non‑diagnostic and not intended for human or animal administration.*

Our Research

We undertake formulation and stability studies for small‑molecule compounds to help scientists and innovators explore new possibilities.

  • Dissolution profiling: evaluating how compounds dissolve in various solvent systems to understand their behaviour in different environments.
  • Stability testing: assessing compound stability under varying storage conditions and over extended periods of time.
  • Solubility and precipitation analysis: characterizing solubility limits, precipitation tendencies and degradation behaviour to support development and storage strategies.

Advancing Small‑Molecule Science

Welcome to Atomic Frenzy. We are a private research institute dedicated to analytical chemistry and formulation science. Our mission is to understand the stability, solubility and other properties of small‑molecule compounds and to share this knowledge with the broader scientific community.
